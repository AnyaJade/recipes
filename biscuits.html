Almond cookies

60ml aquafaba
225g ground almonds
160g caster sugar
almond extract
flaked almonds

180°
13-15mins
24 cookies

Method

  1. Mix sugar and ground almonds.
  2. Whisk aquafaba with fork until light, frothy and foamy, about 2 mins.
  3. Mix all together.
  4. Roll, then press slightly in flaked almonds, both sides
  5. Bake.

Notes

  • Nice, but make them small since sweet and dry.

Bourbons

200g butter
100g caster sugar
2tbsp cocoa
280g P flour

Filling:
75g butter
100g icing sugar
2tbsp custard powder
2tbsp cocoa powder
30g melted dark chocolate

160°
15-20mins
30 small sandwiches

Method

  1. Cream sugar and butter.
  2. Stir in dry and mix to dough.
  3. Chill 30mins.
  4. Roll to 0.5cm thick, cut, prick, bake.
  5. For filling, cream sugar and butter first.

Notes

  • “Like a bourbon, but good!”;
  • Freezing changes the texture - do not recommend.

Hobnobs

75g butter
75g caster sugar
1/2tbsp syrup
75g wholemeal flour
150g oats
1/2 tsp B of S

180°
10mins
15 cookies

Method

  1. Beat butter and sugar.
  2. Mix in syrup.
  3. Add mixed dry to wet a bit at a time -> crumbly mix.
  4. Press mixture into a 6.5cm cookie cutter and compress
  5. Bake and top.

Notes

  • A bit too sweet, so needs that dark chocolate - could try reducing sugar?
  • Used ~60g chocolate to cover.
  • 2tbsp mix per biscuit.
  • Like crumble mix.

Lebkuchen

7oz runny honey
3oz butter
7oz P flour
3.5oz ground almonds
1tsp BP
1/4tsp B of S
1.5tsp cinnamon
1.5tsp ginger
1tsp mixed spice
(0.5tsp nutmeg/allspice/..)
half zest and juice of orange
200g chocolate

180°
15mins
18 cookies

Method

  1. Melt honey and butter, then cool 5 mins in fridge.
  2. Add remaining then chill for at least an hour.
  3. Roll with damp hands.
  4. Bake, cool, dip.

Notes

  • “Like a jaffa cake”.
  • Used 108g chocolate to coat bottoms

Lemon sandwich biscuits

150g butter
1 lemon zest
85g caster sugar
1 egg yolk (optional)
280g P flour
1tbsp cornflour

Filling:
75g butter
130g icing sugar
2tbsp lemon curd.

180°
14-16mins
20 sandwiches

Method

  1. Cream sugar, butter, zest 2-3 mins until pale and creamy.
  2. Beat in egg yolk 1 min.; Mix in flour -> clumps.
  3. Bring to ball, roll to 3-4mm thick, chill at least an hour.
  4. Cut, bake.
  5. Beat buttercream 3-5mins until very pale and creamy before adding lemon curd.

Notes

  • Can top with simple lemon icing.

Nankhatai

3 green cardamom pods
40g icing sugar
100g butter
70g SR flour
25g semolina
25g gram flour
pinch salt
almonds

140°
20mins
24 cookies

Method

  1. Grind cardamom seeds with 1tsp icing sugar.
  2. Beat butter, sugar, cardamom until light and creamy about 10mins.
  3. Marble-sized balls.
  4. Flatten slightly with almond.
  5. Chill 20 mins before baking.

Notes

  • Delicate flavour and light biscuit.

Viennese biscuits

50g icing sugar
