2.1 Bourbons

200g butter
100g caster sugar
2tbsp cocoa
280g P flour

Filling:
75g butter
100g icing sugar
2tbsp custard powder
2tbsp cocoa powder
30g melted dark chocolate

160C

15-20 mins

18 x fingers or 30 x small rounds

Method

- Cream sugar and butter.
- Stir in dry and mix to dough.
- Chill 30mins.
- Roll to 0.5cm thick, cut, prick, bake. For filling:
- Cream sugar and butter first.

Notes

- "Like a bourbon, but good!"
- Freezing changes the texture.

2.10 Hobnobs

75g butter
75g caster sugar
1/2tbsp syrup
75g wholemeal flour
150g oats
1/2 tsp B of S

180C

10 mins

15

Method

- Beat butter and sugar.
- Mix in syrup.
- Add mixed dry to wet a bit at a time -> crumbly mix.
- Press mixture into a 6.5cm cookie cutter and compress;, Bake and spread.

Notes

- A bit too sweet, so needs that dark chocolate - could try reducing sugar?
- Used ~60g chocolate to cover.
- 2tbsp mix per biscuit.
- Like crumble mix.

2.11 Lebkuchen

7oz runny honey
3oz butter
7oz P flour
3.5oz ground almonds
1tsp BP
1/4tsp B of S
3/2 tsp cinnamon
3.5tsp ginger
1tsp mixed spice
(1/2tsp nutmeg/allspice/..)
1/2 zest and juice of orange
200g chocolate

180C

15 mins

18

Method

- Melt honey and butter, then cool 5 mins in fridge.
- Add remaining then chill for at least an hour.
- Roll with damp hands.
- Bake, cool, dip.

Notes

- "Like a jaffa cake".
- Used 108g chocolate to coat bottoms

2.12 Lemon Sandwich Biscuits

150g butter
1 lemon zest
85g caster sugar
1 egg yolk
280g P flour
1tbsp cornflour

Filling:
75g butter
130g icing sugar
2tbsp lemon curd

180C

14-16 mins

20 x sandwiches

Method

- Cream sugar, butter, zest 2-3 mins until pale and creamy.
- Beat in egg yolk 1 min.
- Mix in flour -> clumps.
- Bring to ball, roll to 3-4mm thick, chill at least an hour.
- Cut, bake.
- Beat buttercream 3-5mins until very pale and creamy before adding lemon curd.

Notes

- Can top with simple lemon icing.

2.13 Nankhatai

3 green cardamom pods
40g icing sugar
100g butter
70g SR flour
25g semolina
25g gram flour
pinch salt
almonds

140C

20 mins

24

Method

- Grind cardamom seeds with 1tsp icing sugar.
- Beat butter, sugar, cardamom until light and creamy about 10mins.
- Marble-sized balls.
- Flatten slightly with almond.
- Chill 20 mins before baking.

Notes

- Delicate flavour and light biscuit.
