1oz coconut
1oz walnuts

3.5 Devil’s Food Cake

2oz cocoa
6floz boiling water
6oz marg
12oz dark brown sugar
3 eggs
10oz P flour
3/2tsp B of S
1/4 tsp BP
4floz soured cream/buttermilk

180C

30-35mins

NA x 9” round

Method

- Mix and cool cocoa and water
- Cream marg, sugar and eggs
- Sift dry twice
- Add dry to creamed in 3 batches, alternating with dairy
